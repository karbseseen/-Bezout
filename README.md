# Bézout
Calculates gcd and Bézout coefficients for given numbers

Example:
```
                               5678|1234
                               4936|4
                               ----
                           1234|742
                           742 |1
                           ----
                        742|492
                        492|1
                        ---
                    492|250
                    250|1
                    ---
                250|242
                242|1
                ---
              242|8
              24 |30
                2
                0
              ---
              8|2
              8|4
              -
              0

      j      0      1      2      3      4      5      6
     Qj      -      4      1      1      1      1     30
     Kj      1      4      5      9     14     23    704
   Kj-1      -      1      1      2      3      5    153

v = (-1) ^ (6-1) * 153 = -153
u = (-1) ^ 6 * 704 = 704
GCD = -153 * 5678 + 704 * 1234 = 2
```
